What is SinEo Blockchain?

신어코인은 최첨단의 기술력과 노하우를 보유한 생체신호 인식기술과 융합되어 인체에 삽입되거나 신분을 증명하는 휴대폰 등의 보조기구를 사용하는 종래의 기술들과 달리, 생체신호를 인식하는 단말기를 통해 확인된 신원정보를 활용하여 토큰의 보유량, 거래 및 교환 등의 거래 활성화로 참여 산업 간의 유기적 협력의 환경을 구축하고 있습니다.

신어코인에 참여를 원하는 사용자는 최초에 본인 신체의 일부를 생체신호인식 단말기를 이용하여 생체신호를 인식시키게 되고, 생체신호 인식 단말기에 의해 판별된 고유정보는 ID로 구분되어 Wallet을 생성한 신어코인 Smartcontract와 연결되어 등록됩니다. 이는 즉, 참여를 원하는 사용자의 생체신호가 Wallet과 동일함을 의미하게 되는 것입니다. 이로써 사용자는 Contract 상에서 임의의 거래가 형성될 때, 생체신호 인식 단말기를 이용하여 인식된 생체신호를 인증으로 활용하여 Wallet에 저장된 코인을 확인 및 거래 등에 활용할 수 있습니다. 디지털화된 자산은 신어코인 Contract와 연결되어 현장에서 단말기를 통하여 확인이 가능하며, 또한 Web이나 App과의 연동 등의 다양한 방식으로도 디지털 자산의 증명의 가능합니다. technology.

Fixing the Engine of Innovation

본 커뮤니티는 다년간에 걸쳐 진행된 오프라인 모임을 통하여 다수의 신기술, 신산업 창조 및 정신문화 육성의 객관적 실적을 제시하였으며, 이를 토대로 회원들 간의 유기적 연결을 더욱 유연하게 제시하기 위하여 온라인 커뮤니티 활성화와 더불어 촉진하기 위한 구체적 방안을 제시합니다. 신어플랫폼 생태계 내에서 유기적 연결을 통해 제시되는 아이디어는 회원들로 구성된 운영위원회를 통하여 전문적인 의견이 제시되고 평가되어 결과물에 대한 구체적 진행방향을 결정하게 됩니다. 이러한 커뮤니티 활동을 통해 저평가 되거나 구현되지 못하고 사양될 수 있는 아이디어를 발굴하여 구현될 수 있도록 지원합니다.

또한, 회원들의 협업에 의해 구현된 아이디어의 지적재산권 확보를 적극적으로 진행하며, 확보된 지적재산권은 플랫폼 생태계에 참여한 회원들이 우선적으로 구매할 수 잇도록 지원합니다. 이를 통해 아이디어 및 의견제시 등의 적극적인 생태계 기여자들에게는 신어코인을 Airdrop할 수 있게 하여, 기여자들에게 인센티브를 제공하고자 합니다. 다만, 모든 회원에게 Airdrop이 진행되는 것은 아니며, 본 커뮤니티의 건전한 생태계 보호 및 발전을 위한 기여도에 따른 회원을 대상으로 진행됩니다.

Universal Tenure

Qualification is not just what you know. Or who you know. Qualification is a constantly-updating consensus about you that is made by a decentralized, disparate whole.

An individual’s relative involvement with, or impact on, the global development of distributed ledger technology is approximated algorithmically, and given a DiscoverScore (DSCORE). DSCORE is a moving aggregate of an individual's diverse experiences, known collaborators, and gained reputation.

Decentralized Talent

In software and technology, there are trends in increasingly lateral solutions for education, recruitment, research and development. Businesses are becoming increasingly dependent on external services from other business and contractors, while at the same time the duration of these relationships are becoming shorter or more concise. The same is true of learning institutions, who are in tight competition with an increasingly open-source world where knowledge is quick and ubiquitous.

This has both caused and been caused by a shift in the demands of the talent as well. Learning and working online are gaining popularity, due to the dramatic increase in flexibility and opportunities available. Outside of in-person workshops and networking events, most Blockchain development occurs over the Internet, relying on the same strengths that are provided by remote learning and freelancing. In our solution, we integrate the remote learning and freelancing models into a single, reciprocal ecosystem.

What is SinEo Blockchain?

SinEo Blockchain is both an educational and professional marketplace helping clients and individuals connect with skilled Blockchain Technology educators, contractors and entrepreneurs. What differentiates us from the other freelance marketplaces is that our platform is specific to Blockchain Technology projects and purposes. Additionally, beyond conventional functions of the platform we apply the transparent and objective DiscoverSCORE validation algorithm: our chief differentiating value and unique application of Blockchain Technology.

Why does this project need Blockchain Technology?

While many are already hard at work building new protocols, SinEo Blockchain has recognized the ‘self-disruptive’ aspect of the technology demands attention. There exists a necessity for the educational and professional marketplace infrastructure that will help the building process become more reliable, efficient, transparent and secure. Without these, the technology will continue to struggle to launch, destabilized by the weight of its own immense potential. We want people to have control of their educational and professional profiles in a decentralized, peer-to-peer environment using our involvement score (DSCore) and determine the path they choose to take with their skills.

Who are your main competitors?

We have competitors in two distinct industries - education and professional marketplaces. From the latter category, recruitment platforms, freelance job sites whereby the former category, e-learning sites, Blockchain code academies will suffer. That being said, these are operating as sole service providers while SinEo Blockchain focuses on providing both e-learning and professional marketplaces for Blockchain Technology - which is a unique approach to solving an emerging problem.

Why would students, educators, contractors and entrepreneurs use SinEo Blockchain instead of other platforms?
Creating a trusted environment

SinEo Blockchain establishes a system that provides order in a leaderless, egalitarian system by using the innovations provided by distributed ledger technology to bring better organization to future thinkers and doers.

Learning vs. Application

Our primary goal is to address waste resulting from disorganization within and between Education & Business. Academia and the workplace are no longer working hand in hand to bridge the gap between learners and workers. We want to be the bridge and give the opportunity to students to progress into service providers within the platform.

Decentralized Talent

Most Blockchain development occurs over the Internet, relying on the same strengths that are provided by remote learning and freelancing. In our solution, we integrate the remote learning and freelancing models into a single, reciprocal ecosystem.
